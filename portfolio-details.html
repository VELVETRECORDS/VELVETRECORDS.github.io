Rising Star EP Launch

label@velvetrecords.fm

Velvet Records collaborated with emerging R&B and pop artists to produce and launch the Rising Star EP, a project showcasing fresh sounds and creative storytelling. Our team handled every stage of the release with precision and artistry. The campaign highlighted Velvet’s dedication to developing new talent and delivering music that resonates with authenticity and style.

The Rising Star EP project was created to spotlight new and emerging talent under Velvet Records. Our goal was to develop a cohesive sound and visual identity that captured the essence of modern R&B and pop. The project included full scale music production, creative direction, photoshoots, and a digital marketing rollout across major streaming platforms.

The main challenge for the Rising Star EP was crafting a distinct artistic identity for each performer while maintaining a unified sound and brand for the project as a whole. Velvet Records aimed to balance creative freedom with commercial appeal—ensuring that every track felt authentic yet polished for mainstream audiences.

Velvet Records approached the Rising Star EP with a collaborative, artist first strategy. We held creative sessions to define each artist’s unique sound and message, then built a cohesive production style that tied the tracks together. Our in house team handled recording, mixing, and visual branding to ensure a consistent, high quality aesthetic.
