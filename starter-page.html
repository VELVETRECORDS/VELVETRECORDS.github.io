Get Started

VELVET RECORDS

Getting started at VELVET RECORDS is simple for passionate musicians ready to take the next step in their career. Whether you’re a solo artist or part of a band, we welcome original talent with a strong creative vision. To begin, artists can submit a demo and short bio through our official website or email us directly with links to their music and social profiles. Our A&R team reviews every submission carefully, looking for authenticity, originality, and potential for growth. Once selected, artists are invited to collaborate with our producers, creative directors, and marketing specialists to develop their sound, image, and release strategy—turning raw talent into a professional music journey.

Our Starter Section

To get in touch with Velvet Records, artists, collaborators, and fans can reach out through multiple channels. The easiest way is by visiting our Contact page, where you can fill out a short form detailing your inquiry—whether it’s about partnerships, artist submissions, media requests, or event bookings. You can also email us directly at contact@velvetrecords.fm for professional inquiries.

VELVET RECORDS

VELVET RECORDS is an independent music label helping artists shape their sound and share their music with the world. Our Denver roots inspire everything we do, from our collaborative studio sessions to the local events that connect artists and fans across the city.
