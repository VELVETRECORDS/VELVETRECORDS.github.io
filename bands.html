VELVET RECORDS

Neon Drive Album Launch

label@velvetrecords.fm

Velvet Records partnered with Neon Drive, a Denver-based alternative rock band known for their electrifying performances and nostalgic yet modern sound. Together, we produced and launched their debut album, Neon Nights, a project that captures the raw energy of live rock with a polished studio edge. From concept development to the final release, our team worked closely with the band to shape a record that feels both timeless and fresh.

The Neon Nights album was crafted to highlight the band’s dynamic chemistry and evolving style. Velvet Records guided Neon Drive through every stage of production—songwriting, recording, mixing, and visual design—to create an immersive listening experience. The project also included a photo series, live studio sessions, and a digital launch strategy across major streaming platforms.

The main challenge for the Neon Nights album was capturing the raw, live energy that defines Neon Drive’s performances while translating it into a polished studio sound. Velvet Records aimed to preserve the band’s authentic edge—the grit, emotion, and chemistry that make their shows unforgettable—without losing clarity or cohesion in production. Balancing the organic feel of live instruments with modern recording techniques required precision and collaboration.

Velvet Records approached the Neon Nights album with a collaborative, band-focused strategy. We worked closely with Neon Drive to capture their live performance energy in the studio, blending analog warmth with modern production techniques. Our team facilitated creative sessions to refine song arrangements, vocal harmonies, and instrumental dynamics—ensuring every track reflected the band’s signature sound.
